Privacy Policy

PulseFlash is an independent companion app that connects to the local WhatPulse Client API on your device.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or usage data. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

API Usage

PulseFlash connects directly to the local WhatPulse Client API (running on your own machine) to fetch live and unpulsed statistics, including keystrokes, clicks, bandwidth, uptime, and system metrics.

No login, cloud sync, or transmission of private information to external servers occurs at any time.

Third-Party Policy

PulseFlash relies solely on the official WhatPulse client running locally. For more details about WhatPulse and their own data policies, visit:

https://whatpulse.org/privacy

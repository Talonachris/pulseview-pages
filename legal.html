Privacy Policy

PulseView is an independent companion app for viewing and comparing public WhatPulse statistics.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store or transmit any personal data. All usage is local to your device.

API Usage

PulseView connects to the public WhatPulse API to retrieve public statistics (keystrokes, clicks, etc.) of users you choose to view.

No account login, email, or private information is ever requested or accessed by this app.

Third-Party Policy

For more information about WhatPulse and their data practices, please refer to their official privacy policy:

https://whatpulse.org/privacy
